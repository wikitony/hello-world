# mod for factorio
-- Creates early technologies to give a boost to jump straight to space-age
## must have for space
- mining entities
- belts, spliters, underground, loaders/arms
- poles, high poles, substations
- oil processing, chemical plants and refineries
- railroad, trains and station 
- assembly machine 2 and electric smelters
- roboports, bots, chests
- energy source (nuclear or steam)
## for better tiling
- better electric pole ranges for legendary or rare
- better substation ranges for legendary or rare
- better ranges for roboports
